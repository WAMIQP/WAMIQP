about

This web-based application illustrates the geographic and chronological relationships of the helmets. You can drag the timeline and see how the map changes accordingly. You can also slide up to see the full timeline with all the helmets. Find any helmet interesting? Hit its picture on the side and read more about it!

about the collection

This collection consists of 13 helmets originated from Europe and Asia, dating back to as far as 550 B.C. The collection originally resided in Higgins Armory Museum which closed in December 2013. With its close, the collection along with other 2,000 artifacts moved into Worcester Art Museum.

about the project

This application was developed by an Interactive Qualifying Project team from Worcester Polytechnic Institute. The ultimate goal of the project is to enhance the visitor experience with the armory collection at Worcester Art Museum. The project kicked off in November 2013, and concluded in April 2014.
